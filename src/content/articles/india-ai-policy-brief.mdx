## What happened

National AI missions and private cloud investments expanded through 2025–2026, with emphasis on sovereign compute and application layers.

## Why it matters

India's scale in software talent and data regulation choices affects **global supply chains** for services and model deployment — not only domestic markets.

## Technical context

Policy levers include export controls on chips, data localization rules, and public–private cluster partnerships.

## What to watch next

Whether domestic foundation-model training reaches frontier scale or remains application/integration focused.
