## The claim

Research quality is as much institutional as algorithmic.

## The context

Hype cycles reward speed of announcement over reproducibility.

## The technical core

Internal review, negative results, and benchmark discipline separate lasting work from marketing.

## The strongest objection

Commercial pressure necessarily compresses timelines; culture cannot fully resist incentives.

## What comes next

Independent publications and open evaluation may partially substitute for closed lab norms.
