## What happened

Training runs increasingly publicize cluster scale — GPU generations, network topology, and power envelopes.

## Why it matters

Compute availability shapes **who can participate** in frontier research and which algorithmic ideas get tested at scale.

## Technical context

All-reduce bandwidth, HBM capacity, and fault tolerance at thousands of GPUs often dominate wall-clock more than a clever loss tweak.

## What to watch next

Inference-scale serving costs versus pretraining — capex may shift toward deployment efficiency.
