## The problem

Models are marketed with composite scores. Readers need a map of **what each family actually tests**.

## Major camps / approaches

**Knowledge & reasoning suites** — MMLU-style breadth, math, code. **Interactive / agentic** — multi-step tool use. **Safety** — refusal, jailbreak robustness, bias probes. **Process** — chain verification, consistency under perturbation.

## Open questions

Can we standardize **adaptive** evals that resist memorization? How do we evaluate **long-horizon** reliability cheaply?

## Reading path

Start with *Benchmarks That Matter*, then follow primary sources for any model you are assessing.
