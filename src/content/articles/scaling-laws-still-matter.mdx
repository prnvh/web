## What happened

New frontier runs continue to report predictable loss improvements with scale — even as product teams emphasize agents and tools.

## Why it matters

Scaling laws are the **backbone budget justification** for clusters. They are not a theory of alignment, safety, or product-market fit.

## Technical context

Chinchilla-style analysis shifted optimal token-to-parameter ratios. Hardware roadmaps (HBM, interconnect) often bind before algorithmic novelty.

## What to watch next

Whether post-training and inference economics dominate capex decisions more than pretraining scale-ups.
