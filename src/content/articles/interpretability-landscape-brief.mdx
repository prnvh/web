## What happened

Mechanistic interpretability teams publish circuit-level analyses on small models; frontier labs emphasize monitoring and evals at scale.

## Why it matters

Safety and deployment decisions often assume we can **inspect** failures. Interpretability quality determines whether that assumption is honest.

## Technical context

Probing, attribution, sparse autoencoders, and circuit tracing each capture different failure modes — none alone is sufficient.

## What to watch next

Whether interpretability tools influence **training** (not just post-hoc reports) for deployed systems.
